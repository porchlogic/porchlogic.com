The porchLogic Troupe is a mobile 3D sound system for street performances

1. Artists login to the secure portal at backstage.porchlogic.com to upload the individual components (stems) of their tracks.


2. Each stem is assigned to a speaker type:

[kick, snare, hats, bass, pads/chords, melody]


3. The troupe riders select which types they want their speaker to play


4. Artist name and link is displayed on the poster as we ride and play their music
