Typography

Condensed, high-impact headings combined with clean grotesk body text. All caps are reserved for navigation, buttons, and hero copy.

Body

This is the primary body style. It balances a neutral tone with strong legibility. Line lengths should stay between 60–80 characters for comfortable reading.

Muted body copy is used for helper text, product metadata, and secondary descriptions.

Label / UI Text

Caption and meta text live at 12px–13px and should avoid long sentences. Use for prices, timestamps, or category labels.

Buttons & Controls

CTAs use the neon accent with rounded pills. Secondary actions are outlined or ghost to keep focus on the primary path.
