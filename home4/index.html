A mobile, distributed 3D sound system hosting street performances

System Overview

Artists can login to our secure portal at backstage.porchlogic.com to upload the individual components (stems) of their tracks.

Each stem is assigned to a speaker type:

  • Kick
  • Snare
  • Hats
  • Bass
  • Pads/Chords
  • Melody

The troupe riders select which types they want their speaker to play, and the DJ/Artist controls the following parameters:

Parameters

  • Volume
  • Speed
  • Key/Scale
